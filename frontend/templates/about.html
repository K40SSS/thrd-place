Reclaim Your Third Place

In a world where genuine connection is fading, THRDPLACE brings back the community spaces students need to thrive—places that aren't home, aren't class, but somewhere meaningful in between.

The Problem

It's 10 PM on a Tuesday. You have a calculus exam in two days, and none of your friends are available to study. You could go it alone—or you could open THRDPLACE and find someone else who's preparing for the same test, facing the same challenges, ready to meet up and tackle it together.

Traditional social media connects us virtually, but leaves us isolated physically. Study apps focus on productivity, but forget about community. THRDPLACE bridges that gap—it's where purpose meets people.

How It Works

1. Create or Join

Browse study sessions tailored to your courses and schedule, or create your own. Whether it's an in-person meetup at the library, a coffee shop session, or a virtual study room for commuters—you choose what works for you.

2. Connect Instantly

Once you join, you're automatically added to a group chat with session members. Coordinate details, share resources, or just break the ice before you meet.

3. Build Community

Sessions are localized to your school—no strangers from across the country, just fellow students who understand your campus, your courses, and your struggles.

Why "Third Place"?

The term "third place" describes the social spaces distinct from home (first place) and work/school (second place)—cafes, parks, community centers where people naturally gather. These spaces have been disappearing from modern life, taking genuine human connection with them. THRDPLACE revives this concept for students, making it easier than ever to find your people and your place.

Your Campus, Your Community

No more studying alone. No more wishing you had someone to bounce ideas off of. No more feeling disconnected from the thousands of students around you facing the exact same challenges.
